Community Communications Technology Trust

We are reassembling and reconfiguring old computers from the polytechnic supply which is then donated through Com2Tech charity to communities, families, and schools. This is a non rostered volunteer task that is carried out every friday under Tim's supervision. 

This is going to be an ongoing task that will carry over to the next semester until we run out of donated computers.
