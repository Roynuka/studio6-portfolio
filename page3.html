Com2Tech

Community Communications Technology Trust

Com2Tech is a Dunedin based charity that has been working alongside the Otago Polytehnic BIT in the past. This semester Finn was approached by Hamish Smith asking if he was able to round up a few students to help out repurposing unused PCs to donate to the community. This would be a weekly task  with supervision from the Com2Tech guys. At first I was unable to attend some sessions as it coincided with my part-time job on Fridays. But i was able to make time during the week so I could attend. 

The job would take place every Friday from 3pm-5pm. The process is fairly simple as it was just a matter of turning up and setting up some monitors and its peripherals to reinstall windows 10. An air compressor was also provided so we could clean the kit once the installation was complete. 

Some sessions we worked alongside some Level 3-4 CIT certificate students. Those that attended used this time to work on their labs or their assessments as Hamish also taught them. 

A write up/guideline of what to expect of the task is available on the teams resources under filles tab in the DevOps Documentation>Guides folder.

I found this task to be rewarding as I was still unfamiliar with the hardware side of things especially troubelshooting hardware issues. Some sessions had problems we hadn't faced previously so it was interesting to see how experienced I.T. technicians solved them.
